Q. What is the warranty policy of the Benjemer Machine Shop?

A. All works and parts come with a warranty, which can vary from 6 months to 3 years depending on the make and model you select. We are experts at repairing and servicing ball machines and can therefore offer you a level of service that most other retail outlets cannot provide. We are open 7 days per week and can therefore offer you phone support during this time if you need urgent help. We will make sure that all parts and labour are covered for your repair whilst under warranty. If the machine is required to go back to the Australian distributor, we will follow up with them to make sure that your repair is taken care of as quickly and as smoothly as possible. In most cases, you will be required to pay for the postage, however with brands like Lobster and Spinfire, the postage costs are covered by the distributor while under warranty.

Q. Where is manufacturing done?

A. Our manufacturing plant is located in Caloocan, City where we have both 2D and 3D CNC machining capability, finishing processes, inspection, packing and shipping. We also use quality partner suppliers.

Q. What is the turnaround time to get my part finished?

A. Turnaround time can vary based on the complexity of the work required. We can estimate completion time after the part has arrived at our shop, and we have completed an initial inspection of the part.

Q. Can I get a sample made before a full run?

A.Due to the setup time and costs involved in making a sample you would need to place an order for one or a few parts in order to verify a design.
